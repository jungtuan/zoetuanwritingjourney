Hello!

Photo of me taken by Fan Chi Chen. 2018, Taipei, Taiwan.

My name is Zoe Tuan, and I am a professional writer in Taiwan. I published my first book Pink Ink in 2017 when I was in high school, and have continued to engaging in writing activities until now. So far I have published three books, founded a magazine, written passages for several literature magazines, judged a couple literature awards, and attracted 8000+ followers for my instagram account. This website is a journal of my writing career, hope you enjoy it!
